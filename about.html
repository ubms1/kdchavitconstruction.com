About Us

Who We Are

KDChavit Construction is a PCAB-licensed construction firm proudly based in Tuguegarao City, Cagayan. Established in 2026, we have rapidly built a reputation for delivering structurally sound, aesthetically refined, and on-time construction projects.

With a highly skilled team of engineers, architects, and construction professionals, we specialize in Telco Tower Construction and a broad range of engineering structures — from residential homes to large-scale commercial and institutional buildings.

  • Ongoing ISO Certification
  • Expert Engineers & Architects
  • Serving Cagayan Valley & Beyond
  • On-time, On-budget Delivery
  • Safety-First Culture
