Service 01

Telco Tower Construction

Our flagship specialty — KDChavit Construction designs, fabricates, and erects telecommunications towers of all types to the highest structural and safety standards. We work closely with major telecom carriers across the Philippines to expand network coverage in Cagayan Valley and beyond.

  • Monopole, Guyed, and Lattice Tower Structures
  • Tower Foundation Design & Engineering
  • Antenna Mounting & Equipment Installation
  • Tower Inspection, Maintenance & Reinforcement
  • Compliance with DICT and CAAP Regulations
  • Complete Permitting Assistance
