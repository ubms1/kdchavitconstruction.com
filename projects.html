How We Work

Our Construction Process

From initial consultation to project turnover, we follow a proven, transparent process that keeps clients informed and projects on track.

01

Consultation & Site Assessment

We begin with a thorough client consultation to understand your goals, budget, and timeline. A site visit and feasibility assessment are conducted to identify key engineering and design requirements.

02

Design & Engineering

Our architects and engineers develop detailed architectural drawings, structural plans, and engineering specifications. All designs comply with national building codes and local regulations.

03

Permitting & Approvals

We manage all necessary permits and approvals — from building permits and barangay clearances to environmental compliance certificates — so you don't have to navigate the bureaucracy alone.

04

Construction & Quality Control

Construction begins with strict adherence to approved plans and quality control protocols. Regular progress reports and site inspections keep the project on schedule and within budget.

05

Inspection & Testing

Before project completion, all structural elements, MEP systems, and finishes undergo comprehensive inspection and testing to ensure full compliance with design specifications and safety standards.

06

Turnover & After-Sales Support

We formally hand over the completed project with full documentation, warranties, and as-built drawings. Our team remains available for after-sales support, maintenance, and future project phases.
